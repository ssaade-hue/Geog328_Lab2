The Pioneer DJ DDJ-FLX4 is a compact 2-channel DJ controller designed for beginners and hobbyists. It offers an intuitive layout, performance pads, dedicated effects controls, and broad compatibility with popular DJ software. The controller features high-quality jog wheels, a straightforward mixer section, and crossfader assignability for creative transitions.

Key specifications

  • Channels: 2
  • Performance pads: 8 per deck (hot cues, pad FX)
  • Compatibility: Serato, rekordbox, and other popular DJ apps (check software version requirements)
  • Connectivity: USB for computer connection, RCA/master outputs
  • Target audience: Beginner to intermediate DJs
