Pioneer DJ (brief)

Pioneer DJ is a brand of AlphaTheta (formerly a division of Pioneer Corporation) that specializes in DJ equipment — controllers, mixers, and media players — used by DJs and producers worldwide. The DDJ-FLX4 is an entry-level 2-channel DJ controller aimed at hobbyists and beginners; it includes performance pads, effects controls, and compatibility with popular DJ software.
